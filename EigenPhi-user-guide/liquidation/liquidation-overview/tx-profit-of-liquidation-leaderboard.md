# Tx Profit of Liquidation Leaderboard

**Tx Profit** leaderboard tab shows the top 10 liquidation transactions ranked by profit.&#x20;

Click on the row, and you can examine the details of the [liquidation transaction.](../liquidation-transaction.md)&#x20;

You can also click the Liquidator, Asset, and Protocol to view their details.&#x20;
