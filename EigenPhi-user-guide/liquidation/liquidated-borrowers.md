# Liquidated Borrowers

The **Borrower** page presents its liquidated history.&#x20;

Summary displays rundown data.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (274).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Borrower Behaviors** examines the relationship between the liquidation amount and the counts of liquidations. The figure will also show the borrower's history if he or she appears on more than 1 protocol on a certain day.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (250).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Latest Liquidation** lists all the liquidations done on the current borrower in chronicle order. Other than revenue, liquidation amount, and [liquidation LTV](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/classroom/glossary/liquidation/ltv), the other 4 columns in the list can be examined in detail.

* **Tx** hash takes you to the [liquidation transaction detail](liquidation-transaction.md).&#x20;
* The **Liquidator** address will lead you to the [liquidator detail](liquidator-details.md).
* The **Liquidation Asset** column's token symbols show you the [detailed token profile](../home/universal-search/token-profile.md).
* The **Protocol** column's data takes you to the [Liquidation Protocol Overview](liquidation-protocol-overview.md) page.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (285).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Liquidation Leaderboard** reveals the current borrower's liquidation transactions ranking by the profit for the liquidator, top liquidators ranking by amount, top liquidations ranking by amount, and top liquidated assets ranking by amount.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (282).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Tx Profit** leaderboard tab shows the top liquidation transactions ranked by profit for the liquidator.&#x20;

Click on the row, and you can examine the details of the [liquidation transaction.](liquidation-transaction.md)&#x20;

You can also click the Liquidator, Asset, and Protocol to view their details.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (229).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The **Liquidations** tab displays the top liquidation transactions using Liquidation Amount as the ranking condition. You can also examine the transaction detail, assets, and protocols of these transactions.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (287).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The **Liquidated Assets** tab lists tokens ranked by liquidation amount. Click the asset symbol and name, you can examine [its profile](../home/universal-search/token-profile.md).

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (262).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click the liquidator's address to view [its detail](liquidated-borrowers.md).
