# Malicious Tokens

Malicious Tokens help you identify the tokens with bad intentions and risk types identified by EigenPhi. Click either on the icon of the token or on the token address, and you will be redirected to the [Token detail](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/arbitrage-scan-user-guide/token) page.

The risk type we currently identified is "TAXED," which indicates that these tokens will charge a fee for transfer or transaction without informing the user. And the data of malicious tokens is available for download.
