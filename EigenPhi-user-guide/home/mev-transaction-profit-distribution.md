# MEV Transaction Profit Distribution

The chart displays the profit distribution of MEV transactions.&#x20;

The left vertical axis shows the number of transactions in range($), and the right vertical axis measures the average profit in range($).&#x20;

By hovering on the stacked column, you can see the exact amount.
