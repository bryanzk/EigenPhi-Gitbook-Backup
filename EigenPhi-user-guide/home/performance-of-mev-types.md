# Performance of MEV Types

In this section, data and charts can be sorted by profit or by volume. The data on the left shows the profit/volume of different types of MEV transaction.

The chart on the right shows the trading profit data. The left vertical axis indicates the profit amount, and the right one measures the MEV frequency. You can also check out the composition of every day's profit/volume and different colors stand for different MEV types. Hover on the stacked column, and you can see the exact amount.
