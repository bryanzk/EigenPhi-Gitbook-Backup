# Liquidity Pool Profile

**Summary:** The summary section of this LP shows the token pair as USDC/USDT. The pool belongs to Uniswap V3. The transaction fee and flashloan supporting status are not applicable.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (275).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Performance of MEV Type:** In this chart, you can sort the data by profit or volume to show the profit/volume of different MEV transaction types occurring in this pool.    The left vertical axis indicates the profit/volume, and the right vertical axis measures the MEV frequency. You can also check out the composition of every day's profit/volume. The different colors stand for different MEV types. By hovering on the stacked column, you can see the exact amount. &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (298).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Latest MEV:** Like the MEV Live-stream data on the homepage, this section shows the latest MEV transactions occurring in this pool. You can click the transaction hash to view its details.     &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (337).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**MEV Proft Leaderboard:** This section shows the MEV transactions with the highest profit/ROI (click to sort by profit/ROI) occurring in this pool, and you can see their types, contracts, profit, ROI, and time. Click on a specific transaction to get more details.
