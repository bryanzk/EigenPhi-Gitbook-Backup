# Retail Investor

The biggest fear of a retail investor is losing money to scammers, whether it's through questionable tokens or being sandwiched when trading high-volume assets. [Sandwich arbitrage](https://eigenphi.io/ethereum/sandwich) module and [malicious token](https://eigenphi.io/ethereum/pool/malicious) list from EigenPhi can give you a heads up.

Aside from that, you'll want to follow in the footsteps of the alpha traders to reap the benefits. The contract, transaction, and token information pages provide examples for you to follow.
