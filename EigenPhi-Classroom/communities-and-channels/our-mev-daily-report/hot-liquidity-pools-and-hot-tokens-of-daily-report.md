# Hot Liquidity Pools and Hot Tokens of Daily Report

If you are a searcher looking for opportunities, it’s almost impossible to filter the profitable liquidity pools and token pairs without a proper guide. That’s why we provide [Hot Liquidity Pools](https://eigenphi.io/ethereum/pools/hot) and [Hot Tokens](https://eigenphi.io/ethereum/tokens/hot) on https://eigenphi.io/.&#x20;

We selected the top 3 of the Hot LPs and Hot Tokens and put them into the daily report, both on Ethereum and BNB Chain, based on the [trading volume change %](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/classroom/glossary/trading-volume-change).&#x20;

The trading volume of the selected Hot Liquidity Pools has to be larger than $1 million, Hot Tokens $100 thousand.&#x20;

Click either the token symbols or the address. You will be directed to the [token details page](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/arbitrage-scan-user-guide/token) and [liquidity pool pages](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/arbitrage-scan-user-guide/liquidity-pool) for more details to benefit your trade, including other pools having the tokens, MEV profit leaderboard, etc.&#x20;
