# Summary Data by MEV Types of Daily Report

The summary data part of the report demonstrates the rundown of MEV happening on Ethereum during the time period.​

![](https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FR3c3TlxEVFjceBHqcmyp%2Fuploads%2FaPOGy1KoS0Konmo08XhT%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=f854ab8d-844f-4824-b5ea-cc7381bceeb2)

Four donut charts here illustrate the subtotal of profit, cost, revenue, and trading volume of the two MEV types: arbitrage and Sandwich.

The table below the donut charts displays the exact data with the change percentage against yesterday. Take the volume, for example.&#x20;

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png>)

During the period, the overall arbitrage volume is $74,472,495.00, lowering 29.48% against yesterday. While trading volume targeted by Sandwich MEV reaches $762,618,930.00, which increases 49.1% from yesterday. Add them all and you get the total MEV volume of the day: $837,091,424.64, an increasing 35.65% from yesterday.

You can also read the Ethereum MEV market's overall profit, cost, revenue, and ROI trends in the table. The _>1k Tx Count, MEV-Bot Tx Count,_ and _Tx Count_ columns show the MEV frequency data.&#x20;
