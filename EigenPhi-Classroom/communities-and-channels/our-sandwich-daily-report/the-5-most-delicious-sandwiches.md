# The 5 Most Delicious Sandwiches

After the distribution comparison charts and tables, the following table displays the top 5 most profitable Sandwich MEVs. Tokens involved in the MEVs are in one column. Clicking the hash addresses of the MEV will lead you to the [Sandwich detail](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/arbitrage-scan-user-guide/transaction/sandwich-mev-details) page, showing how the sandwich was cooked. You can also click the contract address to see[ the detail of the contract](https://eigenphi-1.gitbook.io/arbitrage-scan-user-guide/contract) used by the sandwich, showing different MEV types’ performance, profit leaderboard, favorite pools, tokens, and profit distribution.
