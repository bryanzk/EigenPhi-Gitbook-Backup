# Download And Share Sandwich Daily Report Data

To download the data, go to the header of the report and click the Download icon.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (9).png>)

You can choose to download the current report's data in the format of either PDF or CSV. Interested in more historical data? Wanna find out the trends over a longer period? Download HIstory Data option will give you a CSV file containing data starting from Jan 1st, 2022.&#x20;

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (14).png>)

Of course, you can share the report via Twitter, Telegram, and Reddit.&#x20;

That’s it for the Ethereum Sandwich MEV daily reports. We have generated them daily since the beginning of 2022, which you can examine in the [Overview section](overview-section-of-sandwich-daily-report.md).&#x20;

If you think we are missing some data you need, please feel free to leave your comment or contact us via social channels. Any feedback is highly encouraged and welcomed!!
